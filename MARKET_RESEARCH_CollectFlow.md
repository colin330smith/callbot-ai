## 9. Strategic Recommendations

### To Hit Aggressive Targets

1. **Pre-validate aggressively** - Get 50 LOIs before writing code
2. **Price higher** - Start at $149/mo, not $99
3. **Obsess over churn** - Build retention features into MVP
4. **Qualify ruthlessly** - Only sell to businesses using QuickBooks/Jobber already
5. **Channel partnerships** - Bookkeepers, accountants = lower CAC
6. **Prove ROI fast** - Dashboard showing "money collected" vs "cost" in week 1

### To Mitigate Churn

1. **90-day onboarding program** - 43% of churn happens here
2. **Weekly ROI emails** - "You collected $X this week"
3. **Annual contracts** - Offer 2 months free for annual
4. **Integration depth** - More integrations = higher switching costs
5. **Community** - Home service business owner community/Slack

### To Reduce CAC

1. **QuickBooks Marketplace** - Organic discovery, lower CAC
2. **Partner channel** - Bookkeepers, accountants (20% rev share)
3. **Referral program** - 1 month free (effectively $149 CAC)
4. **Content/SEO** - Long-term but $150-200 CAC once established
5. **Avoid outbound initially** - $1,980 CAC is too high

## 10. Final Verdict

### Is CollectFlow Viable?

**YES, but with adjusted expectations.**

| Criteria | Assessment |
|----------|------------|
| Problem-market fit | ‚úÖ Strong |
| Business model viability | ‚úÖ Strong |
| $100K MRR Year 1 | ‚ö†Ô∏è Unlikely (realistic: $65-75K) |
| $100K MRR Year 2 | ‚úÖ Achievable |
| $500K MRR Year 5 | ‚úÖ Achievable with execution |
| Solo founder feasible | ‚úÖ Yes, for Year 1 |
| Low investment start | ‚úÖ Yes (<$5K to MVP) |
| High margins | ‚úÖ Yes (80%+) |

### Recommended Next Steps

1. **Validate demand** - Cold email 100 home service businesses, get 10 discovery calls
2. **Test pricing** - Can you get 5 businesses to pre-pay $149/mo?
3. **Build MVP** - 6-8 weeks with Claude Code
4. **Launch on QuickBooks Marketplace** - Lowest CAC channel
5. **Measure churn weekly** - If >7% monthly after Month 3, pivot positioning

### The Honest Assessment

The original $100K MRR in Year 1 projection was **optimistic by ~35%**. With realistic SMB churn (30-50% annual) and CAC ($300-600), expect:

- **Year 1:** $65-75K MRR (not $100K)
- **Year 2:** $120-150K MRR (catch-up growth)
- **Year 5:** $500K MRR achievable with strong execution

This is still a strong business opportunity. It's just not a rocket ship in Year 1.
