Frequently asked questions

How quickly can I get started?

Most businesses are live in under 10 minutes. Just answer a few questions about your business, choose your settings, and you're ready to receive calls.

Do I need to change my phone number?

No. You keep your existing business number. Simply forward calls to your AI when you're unavailable, or let AI handle all calls—your choice.

What happens if the AI can't answer a question?

The AI will take a message with the caller's details and notify you immediately. You can call them back with the information they need.

Can the AI book appointments?

Yes. The AI collects the caller's name, phone number, service needed, and preferred time, then sends you the details instantly.

What if I go over my minutes?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. Additional minutes are billed at a per-minute rate, or you can upgrade your plan anytime.

Is there a contract?

No contracts. Pay month-to-month and cancel anytime. We also offer a 7-day free trial so you can test everything before committing.
