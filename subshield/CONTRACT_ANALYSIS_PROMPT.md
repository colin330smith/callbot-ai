## Your Analysis Instructions

Produce a report with the following sections:

### 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
- Overall Risk Score: [1-10, where 10 is extremely risky]
- One paragraph summary of the contract's fairness to the subcontractor
- Top 3 issues that need immediate attention

### 2. PAYMENT TERMS ANALYSIS

#### Pay-If-Paid vs Pay-When-Paid
- Quote the exact language from the contract
- Explain what it means in plain English
- Risk level: HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW
- State-specific note: Is this enforceable in [STATE]?
- Negotiation script if problematic

#### Payment Timeline
- When are invoices due?
- When is payment due after invoice?
- Are there any conditions that could delay payment?
- Risk level and recommendations

#### Retainage
- What percentage is being held?
- When is it released?
- Are there conditions for release?
- Risk level and recommendations

### 3. LIEN RIGHTS ANALYSIS

#### Lien Waiver Requirements
- What type of waivers are required? (Conditional vs Unconditional)
- When must they be submitted?
- Quote problematic language
- Risk level: HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW
- State-specific note: Does [STATE] have statutory lien waiver forms?
- Negotiation script if problematic

#### Notice Requirements
- Preliminary notice requirements
- Timeline for filing liens
- Any contract language that could waive lien rights

### 4. INDEMNIFICATION ANALYSIS

#### Scope of Indemnification
- Quote the indemnification clause
- Plain English explanation
- Does it require you to indemnify for the GC's own negligence?
- Risk level: HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW
- State-specific note: Are broad indemnification clauses enforceable in [STATE]?
- Negotiation script if problematic

#### Insurance Requirements
- What coverage is required?
- Are limits reasonable for this trade?
- Additional insured requirements

### 5. CHANGE ORDER PROVISIONS

#### Notice Requirements
- How quickly must you notify of changes?
- What format is required?
- Quote the exact language
- Risk level and recommendations

#### Pricing for Changes
- How are change orders priced?
- Are there markup limitations?
- What if work must proceed before approval?

### 6. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

#### Dispute Process
- Mediation required?
- Arbitration or litigation?
- Venue/jurisdiction
- Attorney fee provisions

#### Claims Timeline
- How quickly must claims be submitted?
- Are there any claim waiver provisions?

### 7. TERMINATION PROVISIONS

#### Termination for Convenience
- Can the GC terminate without cause?
- What compensation do you receive?
- Quote the language

#### Termination for Cause
- What constitutes cause?
- Cure period provided?
- Risk assessment

### 8. OTHER RED FLAGS

#### Liquidated Damages
- Any LD provisions that flow down?
- Daily amount?
- Risk level

#### Warranty Terms
- Duration of warranty
- Scope of warranty
- Call-back provisions

#### Scope Creep Language
- "Including but not limited to" clauses
- Ambiguous scope definitions
- Risk assessment
