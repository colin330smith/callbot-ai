# SUBCONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

**Project:** Sunset Ridge Commercial Center
**Location:** Orlando, Florida
**Date:** January 15, 2025

This Subcontractor Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between:

**GENERAL CONTRACTOR:** Pinnacle Construction Group, LLC ("Contractor")
**SUBCONTRACTOR:** [Customer Company Name] ("Subcontractor")

## ARTICLE 1 - SCOPE OF WORK

1.1 Subcontractor agrees to furnish all labor, materials, equipment, and supervision necessary to complete the electrical installation work as described in the Contract Documents, including but not limited to all work reasonably inferable from the drawings and specifications.

1.2 The scope of work includes, but is not limited to:
- Complete electrical rough-in and finish for Building A and Building B
- Installation of main electrical service and distribution panels
- All lighting fixtures and controls
- Fire alarm system installation
- Low voltage systems including data and telecommunications
- Any additional electrical work required to complete the project

1.3 Subcontractor acknowledges that the scope of work may be modified at the sole discretion of the Contractor to accommodate project requirements.

## ARTICLE 2 - CONTRACT PRICE

2.1 The Contract Price for the Work shall be Three Hundred Forty-Seven Thousand Dollars ($347,000.00).

2.2 The Contract Price includes all costs associated with the Work including labor, materials, equipment, overhead, profit, insurance, taxes, and any other expenses.

## ARTICLE 3 - PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Progress Payments.** Subcontractor shall submit monthly Applications for Payment by the 25th of each month. Contractor shall process approved Applications for Payment within forty-five (45) days of receipt.

3.2 **Condition Precedent to Payment.** It is expressly understood and agreed that Contractor's receipt of payment from Owner for Subcontractor's work is a condition precedent to Contractor's obligation to make payment to Subcontractor. Subcontractor expressly assumes the risk of Owner's nonpayment for any reason whatsoever, and the Subcontract Price includes this assumption of risk. If Owner fails to pay Contractor for Subcontractor's work, Contractor shall have no obligation to pay Subcontractor, regardless of whether Subcontractor has fully performed.

3.3 **Retainage.** Contractor shall retain ten percent (10%) of each progress payment until final completion of the entire Project and receipt of final payment from Owner.

3.4 **Final Payment.** Final payment, including all retainage, shall be made within sixty (60) days after final acceptance of the Work by Owner and Contractor's receipt of final payment from Owner.

## ARTICLE 4 - LIEN WAIVERS

4.1 As a condition precedent to each progress payment, Subcontractor shall provide Contractor with an unconditional waiver and release of lien for all work performed and materials supplied through the date of the previous Application for Payment.

4.2 As a condition precedent to final payment, Subcontractor shall provide Contractor with a final unconditional waiver and release of lien and affidavit stating that all labor, materials, and equipment furnished have been paid in full.

4.3 Subcontractor waives any right to file or assert a mechanic's lien against the Project or any bond related thereto for any amounts owed under this Agreement.

## ARTICLE 5 - CHANGES IN THE WORK

5.1 **Change Orders.** No changes in the Work shall be recognized or paid for unless authorized in writing by Contractor prior to performance of such work.

5.2 **Notice Requirement.** Subcontractor must provide written notice to Contractor within twenty-four (24) hours of discovering any condition that may require a change in the Work or result in additional costs. Failure to provide such notice within the required time shall constitute a waiver of any claim for additional compensation or time extension.

5.3 **Pricing of Changes.** All change order work shall be performed at cost plus ten percent (10%) markup for overhead and profit, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

5.4 **Directed Changes.** Contractor may direct Subcontractor to proceed with changed work pending execution of a formal Change Order. Subcontractor shall proceed with such work and shall not delay progress pending resolution of pricing disputes.

## ARTICLE 6 - INDEMNIFICATION

6.1 To the fullest extent permitted by law, Subcontractor shall defend, indemnify, and hold harmless Contractor, Owner, and their respective officers, directors, employees, and agents from and against any and all claims, damages, losses, costs, and expenses, including but not limited to attorneys' fees, arising out of or resulting from:

(a) The performance of the Work, regardless of whether such claim is caused in whole or in part by the negligence or fault of any party indemnified hereunder;

(b) Any act, omission, or negligence of Subcontractor, its employees, agents, or sub-subcontractors;

(c) Any breach of this Agreement by Subcontractor;

(d) Any violation of applicable laws, codes, or regulations.

6.2 This indemnification obligation shall not be limited by any limitation on the amount or type of damages, compensation, or benefits payable by or for Subcontractor under workers' compensation acts, disability benefit acts, or other employee benefit acts.

## ARTICLE 7 - INSURANCE

7.1 Subcontractor shall maintain the following insurance coverage:

- Commercial General Liability: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $2,000,000 aggregate
- Automobile Liability: $1,000,000 combined single limit
- Workers' Compensation: Statutory limits
- Umbrella/Excess Liability: $2,000,000

7.2 Contractor and Owner shall be named as additional insureds on all liability policies.

7.3 Subcontractor shall provide certificates of insurance prior to commencing work and shall maintain coverage throughout the duration of the warranty period.

## ARTICLE 8 - WARRANTY

8.1 Subcontractor warrants that all Work shall be free from defects in materials and workmanship for a period of two (2) years from the date of substantial completion of the Project.

8.2 Subcontractor shall promptly correct any defective Work at no additional cost to Contractor or Owner upon receipt of written notice.

8.3 Subcontractor shall respond to warranty calls within twenty-four (24) hours and shall complete warranty repairs within seventy-two (72) hours.

## ARTICLE 9 - SCHEDULE

9.1 Time is of the essence. Subcontractor shall commence and complete the Work in accordance with the Project schedule provided by Contractor.

9.2 Subcontractor acknowledges that delay in completion of the Work may result in substantial damages to Contractor and Owner. Subcontractor agrees to pay liquidated damages in the amount of Five Hundred Dollars ($500.00) per calendar day for each day of delay beyond the scheduled completion date, unless such delay is caused solely by Contractor or Owner.

## ARTICLE 10 - TERMINATION

10.1 **Termination for Convenience.** Contractor may terminate this Agreement at any time for convenience upon five (5) days written notice to Subcontractor. In the event of termination for convenience, Subcontractor shall be entitled to payment for Work properly performed prior to termination.

10.2 **Termination for Cause.** Contractor may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if Subcontractor:
(a) Fails to prosecute the Work diligently;
(b) Fails to correct defective Work;
(c) Violates any provision of this Agreement;
(d) Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy.

## ARTICLE 11 - DISPUTE RESOLUTION

11.1 Any dispute arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Orange County, Florida in accordance with the Construction Industry Arbitration Rules of the American Arbitration Association.

11.2 The prevailing party in any dispute shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorneys' fees and costs from the non-prevailing party.

11.3 Subcontractor shall continue to perform the Work during the pendency of any dispute.

## ARTICLE 12 - GENERAL PROVISIONS

12.1 **Independent Contractor.** Subcontractor is an independent contractor and not an employee of Contractor.

12.2 **Assignment.** Subcontractor shall not assign this Agreement without Contractor's prior written consent.

12.3 **Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Florida.

12.4 **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations and agreements.

12.5 **Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is found to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

**IN WITNESS WHEREOF**, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.

**PINNACLE CONSTRUCTION GROUP, LLC**

By: _________________________ Date: _____________
