Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2025

SubShield ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our contract analysis services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and business name
  • Contract Documents: PDF files and documents you submit for analysis
  • Payment Information: Payment details processed securely through Stripe (we do not store full credit card numbers)
  • Communications: Messages, emails, and correspondence with our team

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide contract analysis services you request
  • Deliver reports and communicate about your orders
  • Process payments securely
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer support
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Send important service updates and notifications

3. Contract Document Confidentiality

We understand that your contracts contain sensitive business information. We commit to:

  • Treating all submitted contracts as confidential
  • Using contracts only for the purpose of providing analysis services
  • Not sharing your contracts with third parties except as required by law
  • Implementing appropriate security measures to protect stored documents
  • Retaining contracts only as long as necessary to provide services

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in operating our business (e.g., payment processing through Stripe)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure payment processing through Stripe
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security assessments

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Contract documents are retained for a reasonable period to allow for follow-up questions and then securely deleted.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at colin@trysubshield.com.

8. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience and analyze website traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy
